### Legal Requirements Findings

- **Best Visa Option for Brazilians**: **E-2 Treaty Investor Visa**
  - Requirement: $100,000+ investment in business
  - Processing time: 4-6 weeks
  - Validity: 2 years, renewable indefinitely
  - Alternative: L-1A (if transferring from Brazilian parent company with 1+ year experience)
  - Source: https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/us-visas/business/treaty-investors-e-1-e-2.html

- **Recommended Business Entity**: **LLC (Limited Liability Company)**
  - Florida LLC formation: $125 filing fee, 5 business days processing
  - Tax advantage: Pass-through taxation (simpler than C-Corp)
  - Liability protection: Personal assets protected
  - Source: https://dos.myflorida.com/business-professional/business-corporations/

- **Key Requirements for Foreign-Owned Business in Florida**:
  - EIN from IRS (free)
  - Business bank account (requires EIN + incorporation docs)
  - Registered Agent in Florida (can be third-party service: ~$150/year)
  - Compliance: Quarterly reports, annual renewals
  - Tax filings: Federal (1040-ES quarterly estimates), Florida (annual corporation tax)
  - Source: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/foreign-investors
